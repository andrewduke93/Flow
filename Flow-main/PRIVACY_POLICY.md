# Privacy Policy for Flow

**Last Updated: February 1, 2026**

## Overview

Flow is a book reading application that respects your privacy. All your data stays on your device.

## What We Store Locally

- **Your Books**: EPUB files you import are stored locally on your device only
- **Reading Progress**: Bookmarks, current page positions, and chapter progress
- **Preferences**: Theme, font size, reading speed (WPM), and display settings

## What We DO NOT Do

- ❌ Upload your books to any server
- ❌ Track what you read or how you read
- ❌ Collect personal information
- ❌ Sell or share your data with third parties
- ❌ Require account creation to use the app
- ❌ Display advertisements

## Optional Features

### Google Drive Sync (Opt-in)
If you choose to enable cloud sync:
- Your reading progress and preferences back up to YOUR Google Drive account
- We never access or store your Google Drive data on our servers
- You can disconnect at any time in Settings
- Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Project Gutenberg Library
When browsing free books:
- We query the public Gutendex API to search for books
- No personal data is sent with these requests
- Downloaded books are stored locally on your device

## Data Security

- All data is stored using your device's secure storage mechanisms
- No data is transmitted to our servers
- Optional Google Drive sync uses encrypted HTTPS connections

## Children's Privacy

Flow does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is a general-purpose reading tool suitable for all ages.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete all app data at any time (Settings > Clear Data)
- Export your reading data
- Disconnect Google Drive sync
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data
